Who We Are

Aizawl's Trusted
Auto Care Studio

JMD Auto Care is a vehicle detailing and auto care studio located in Vaivakawn Zohnuai, Aizawl, Mizoram. We combine quality products with skilled hands to give your vehicle the treatment it truly deserves.

We're not your average car wash. Our studio is equipped with professional-grade steam cleaners, ceramic detailing solutions, and a full in-store inventory of the best auto care brands — including Turtle Wax, JOPASU, and Wavex. Whether you drive a hatchback or a large SUV, we treat every vehicle with the same obsessive care.
